About Me

Reading, learning, and making stuff are my favorites. Please scroll below to read my journey from banking to tech. You may find more details in my resume.

My latest learning interests are data science and programming. With these skills, I hope to work on projects relating to disinformation and promoting constructive conversation in democratic societies.

Originally from central Taiwan, our family of four now calls Yilan home because we love the mountains and people in Eastern Taiwan so much. Before the kids, my husband and I enjoy climbing and hiking. Now we are amateur farmers who have more fun playing mud than counting harvest. As a hobby, I make lots of soaps.

Work Experience

2015 - Present Co-founder

Chi Jia Technology Co., Ltd.

Chi-Jia in Mandarin means to harmonize the family. Our eCommerce services were built with the vision to improve the quality of life for each member of the family. lifehappiness.com (2018~present) was born out of a mother’s care to provide safe and durable cookware. With a love for humor, mmwooo.com (2016~2018) introduced fun accessories to our fans to pamper the youngsters and their inner children.

2017 - 2019 E-Commerce Advisor

Y.L Textile Co., Ltd.

Taiwan is known for our strong OEM/ODM capability, yet Taiwanese manufacturers must move up the value chain as we face grimmer competition from countries with lower production costs. Mammam.com was set up as an effort to bridge a traditional fabric factory from its declining B2B business to the fast-growing online B2C market. I built its online presence through various channels and executed digital marketing campaigns. Joining the digital transition for this three-generation enterprise that represents a part of Taiwan history was a truly inspiring experience for me.

2015 - 2019 Co-founder

Kosbrother. Inc.

Fortunately, NovelKing was profitable enough for me to devote full-time to the company. As new parents, my husband and I opted for a virtual organization when building the company. Managing a remote team turned out to be a challenging yet liberating task. Later I launched DoctorGuide with an aim to improve information circulation and administration efficiency of the medical system.

2013 - 2015 Assistant Manager

Taipei Fubon Commercial Bank

To take a step further, I switched to Syndication where the product structure was more complicated and the market was wider, covering the Greater China region. I cooperated with the Hong Kong desk to solicit mandates and attract subscriptions from other financial institutions. At the same time, my interest in the information economy grew. A fervent bookworm myself, I joined my husband in developing NovelKing as a side-project.

2012 - 2013 Associate

Standard Chartered Bank (Taiwan) Limited

While in Standard Chartered, I aided MNC clients for their global capital market activities. On top of that, I formed a keen security awareness learning from the internal control measures in this 87,000-employee enterprise, such as segregation of duties, authorization, and IT controls.

2010 - 2012 Management Associate

Taishin International Bank

Two years in a team with hyper sales growth instilled in me the pragmatic and market-facing approach that benefited me tremendously in later positions and the startup journey. I also learned the concept of balancing risk and return, which I continue to apply in life and business pursuits.
